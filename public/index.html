Open source, private chat goodness.

    Why FreeStep?

    Simple

    With an elegantly minimal interface and drag and drop file uploads, Freestep does what you need without clutter or advertisements.

    Secure

    Using the state of the art Rabbit stream cipher, FreeStep keeps your chats and images private - even from the server.

    Transparent

    FreeStep is entirely open source on GitHub, available for code review or customization under the MIT license. FreeStep never advertises, logs, or tracks - ever.
